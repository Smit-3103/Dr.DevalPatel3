Departments Of Neurology

All Departments › Surgery & Imaging › Neurology

The Neurology Department at Medicoz Hospital is dedicated to the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nervous system, and muscles. Our team of board-certified neurologists and neurosurgeons works collaboratively to provide comprehensive, individualized care to patients with a wide range of neurological conditions.

We offer advanced diagnostic capabilities including MRI, CT, EEG, nerve conduction studies, and lumbar puncture services. Our department is equipped with the latest neuroimaging technology, enabling precise diagnosis and targeted treatment planning for conditions such as stroke, epilepsy, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson's disease, and more.

  • Offering the most specialized neurological surgeons
  • Latest surgical procedures and advanced resources
  • A skilled team of experienced medical specialists
  • Having the latest neurological medical advances
  • 24/7 emergency neurology care and consultation

Why Choose This Service

Our neurology department is led by internationally recognized specialists with decades of combined experience. Every physician on our team is board-certified and committed to staying at the forefront of neurological medicine through ongoing research and continuing education.

With over 15 years serving the community, our neurologists have treated thousands of patients with complex neurological conditions. Our experience translates into better diagnostic accuracy, more effective treatments, and improved patient outcomes across all neurological specialties.

We employ evidence-based practice solutions tailored to each patient's unique clinical profile. From the latest minimally invasive surgical techniques to cutting-edge pharmacological therapies, our practice solutions ensure patients receive the most effective care available.
