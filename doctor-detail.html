Dr. Nathan Hill

MBBS (Sydney), FRACS (Paediatric Surgery)

Dr. Nathan Hill is a highly distinguished Chief Surgeon at Medicoz Hospital, having graduated with honours from the Sydney Medical School at the University of Sydney. He completed his surgical residency and fellowship in paediatric surgery at the Royal Children's Hospital, Melbourne, before joining our team 15 years ago.

Dr. Hill is internationally recognized for his expertise in minimally invasive surgical techniques and has published over 50 peer-reviewed papers in leading medical journals. He has been consistently rated as one of the top surgeons in the region by both peers and patients.
