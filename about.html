ABOUT US

We Provide Best Medical Services

Medicoz Hospital was founded with a singular vision: to deliver healthcare that combines cutting-edge medical science with genuine human compassion. Since our establishment, we have grown into a full-service medical center serving thousands of patients annually across all major medical disciplines.

Our facility spans over 200,000 square feet and houses state-of-the-art diagnostic labs, advanced surgical suites, dedicated intensive care units, and comfortable patient wards. We believe that the environment in which care is delivered is as important as the care itself.

At Medicoz, our multidisciplinary team of over 75 specialist physicians, 200 nurses, and support staff work in concert to ensure that every patient receives comprehensive, coordinated, and personalized medical care from the moment they arrive through their full recovery.

Dr. Emily Haden Chief Medical Officer, Medicoz Hospital
